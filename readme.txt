== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `we-divi-modules` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Extended SM Follow Module' direct from the Divi builder like their offical Module.
